About Code Naman

Code Naman is not just a job portal — it’s an AI-driven platform that connects skilled engineers with verified jobs, freelance projects, and remote opportunities. We eliminate the need for consultancies, fake job listings, and endless follow-ups.

Our goal is to bring transparency, speed, and smart automation into the hiring process — powered by AI, verified profiles, and a smart interview and resume system.

Whether you're a job seeker, freelancer, or recruiter, Code Naman gives you an intelligent edge to find the right match — faster, smarter, and verified.
